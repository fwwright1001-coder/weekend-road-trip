# 30-Second Demo — Recording Guide

A tight gameplay clip is the single highest-ROI portfolio asset (the README hero
should be cut from it). Recording needs a human at the keyboard — here's the
storyboard so it lands the beats in ~30s.

**Capture:** 960×540 (the game's logical size), 60fps. OBS or
ScreenToGif / `ffmpeg` on the browser window. Export an MP4 for LinkedIn/portfolio
and a trimmed GIF (<8MB) for the README.

**Shot list (≈30s):**

1. **0–3s — Title.** Land on the title screen, hit START. (Clean, sets the tone.)
2. **3–10s — Lane weaving (DOWNTOWN).** Weave across all three lanes around a
   wall-with-one-gap pattern; jump a pothole, duck a full-width sign wall. Show
   the second decision axis immediately.
3. **10–18s — Combo build (MUSIC ROW/CUMBERLAND).** Skim adjacent-lane hazards for
   `NEAR MISS` popups and grab cans in risky lanes; let the combo climb past x5
   (aim for x10+) so the multiplier ramp is visible.
4. **18–25s — The climax (BROADWAY).** Show the speed peaking and the densest
   patterns — the finale reading as the hardest leg.
5. **25–30s — Payoff.** Cross the finish → `YOU MADE IT!` stats card.

**Optional B-roll for the case study:** a 5s clip of `node sim/balance-sim.js`
printing `ACCEPTANCE: ALL CRITERIA MET ✔` — pairs well with the ARCHITECTURE.md
fairness section to show "balance is machine-verified, not eyeballed."
